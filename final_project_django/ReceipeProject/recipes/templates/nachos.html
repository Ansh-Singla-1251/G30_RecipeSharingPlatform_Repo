Nachos Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 bag tortilla chips
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1/4 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Spread tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle cheese and black beans over chips.
  4. Bake for 10-15 minutes until cheese melts.
  5. Top with tomatoes and sour cream. Serve hot!
