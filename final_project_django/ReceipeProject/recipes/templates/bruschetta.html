Bruschetta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 2 cups diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Slice baguette and brush with olive oil.
  3. Toast in oven for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Mix tomatoes, garlic, and basil with remaining oil.
  5. Spoon mixture onto toasted bread. Serve immediately!
