Empanadas Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour with water to form dough; let rest.
  2. Cook beef with onion and paprika until browned.
  3. Roll out dough, cut circles, and fill with beef mixture.
  4. Fold and seal edges, then fry in hot oil until golden.
  5. Serve warm. Enjoy!
