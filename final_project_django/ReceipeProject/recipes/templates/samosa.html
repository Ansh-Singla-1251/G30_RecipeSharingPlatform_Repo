Samosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and eggs in a bowl.
  3. Add vanilla extract and milk, stirring until smooth.
  4. Pour mixture into a greased baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Let cool before serving. Enjoy!
