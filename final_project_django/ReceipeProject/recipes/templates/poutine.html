Poutine Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 cups frozen fries
  • 1 cup cheese curds
  • 2 cups beef gravy

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Bake fries according to package instructions.
  3. Heat gravy in a saucepan until simmering.
  4. Place fries on a plate, top with cheese curds, and pour hot gravy over.
  5. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
