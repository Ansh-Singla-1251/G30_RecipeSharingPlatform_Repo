Falafel Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
  2. Blend chickpeas, parsley, garlic, and cumin into a coarse paste.
  3. Form into balls and flatten slightly.
  4. Heat oil and fry until golden brown, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve with tahini sauce. Enjoy!
