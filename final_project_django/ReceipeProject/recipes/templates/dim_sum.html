Dim Sum Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shrimp
  • 1/2 cup bamboo shoots
  • 1 pack wonton wrappers
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Chop shrimp and bamboo shoots finely.
  2. Mix with soy sauce and sesame oil.
  3. Place filling in wonton wrappers and fold into dumplings.
  4. Steam for 10-12 minutes.
  5. Serve with soy dipping sauce. Enjoy!
